Tips: To Do's

Tap the Add button in the menu bar to create a new To Do Group. Optional: Select an icon for the group in the pop-up dialog.

The Edit button in the menu bar toggles between viewing and editing your To Do Groups (i.e. rename, change icon, delete).

The Reorder Groups option in the drop-down menu lets you rearrange groups by dragging and dropping.

Swipe right or tap the Reminders tab to view your Reminders (and see more tips).
