Tips: To Do Lists

Tap the Add button in the menu bar to create a new To Do List. Once created, tap a group to add and checkoff to do items.

The Edit button in the menu bar toggles between view mode and edit mode (e.g. rename, change icon, delete).

The Reorder option in the drop-down menu lets you rearrange lists by dragging and dropping.

Swipe right or tap the Reminders tab to view your Reminders (and see more tips).
