Tips: To Do Lists

Tap the (Add) button in the menu bar to create a new To Do List. Once created, tap a list to manage your to do items.

The (Edit) button in the menu bar toggles between view mode and edit mode (e.g. rename, change icon, delete).

The Reorder option in the drop-down menu lets you rearrange lists by dragging and dropping.

Swipe right or tap the Reminders tab to view your Reminders (and see Reminder tips).

The Friends option will open your Friends list where you can share to do lists and send reminders with other users.

The User Settings option in the drop-down menu allows you to:

  • Edit your Display Name
  • Set the time of day to receive reminder notifications
