Tips: Friends

Tap (Add) in the menu bar to send a friend request. You will receive a notification when they respond.

Sharing and Friend Actions:

  • Tap (Share To Do List) to invite a friend to view and edit a To Do List. This will enable real-time syncing of the list for each user.
  • Tap (Send Reminder) to send a reminder to a friend. If accepted, the reminder will be copied to your friend's account where they can manage it independently.
  • Tap (Remove Friend) to remove a friend from your list.
